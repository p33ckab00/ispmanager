Phase 3B Notes

  • Endpoints can now belong directly to the node or to internal devices inside the node.
  • Premium subscriber mappings can point to an endpoint instead of only storing a manual text label.
  • Active endpoint occupancy is synchronized automatically when a subscriber mapping is saved or removed.
  • PLC devices can now declare a model and auto-generate input and split-output ports.
  • FBT devices can now declare a ratio and auto-generate one input, one primary pass-through output, and one secondary split output.
